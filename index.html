About

I am an M.S. student at the Trustworthy AI Lab, advised by Prof. Jongheon Jeong at Korea University.
My research interests lie in AI safety and security for scalable representations, including self-supervised learning and robust fine-tuning.
More broadly, I hope to contribute to AI systems that remain safe, secure, and trustworthy in practical real-world settings.
