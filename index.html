About

I am an M.S. student at the Trustworthy AI Lab, advised by Prof. Jongheon Jeong at Korea University. My research interests lie in AI safety and security for generative models — including large language models (LLMs), multimodal large language models (MLLMs), and diffusion models — with particular emphasis on alignment and robustness against adversarial and out-of-distribution challenges, as well as AI-generated content detection. Ultimately, my goal is to build AI systems that people can genuinely trust and safely rely on in the real world.

You can find my code on GitHub and my writing on my blog.
